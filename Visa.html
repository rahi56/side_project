Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our visa services.

How long does visa processing usually take?

Processing times vary depending on the destination country and type of visa. Typically, tourist visas take 5-15 working days, while work and student visas may take longer (4-8 weeks). We offer express processing for urgent cases at an additional fee, which can reduce processing time by 30-50%.

What documents do I need to provide?

The required documents vary by visa type and destination country. Generally, you'll need:

  • Valid passport (with at least 6 months validity)
  • Passport-sized photos meeting specific requirements
  • Completed application forms
  • Proof of travel arrangements (flight itinerary, hotel bookings)
  • Financial documents (bank statements, sponsorship letters)
  • For work/student visas: employment offer/acceptance letter

Our consultants will provide you with a complete, customized checklist for your specific case.

Do you guarantee visa approval?

While we cannot guarantee approval as visa decisions are made by government authorities, we significantly increase your chances by ensuring your application is complete, accurate, and meets all requirements. Our experts review all documents before submission to minimize the risk of rejection. Our current success rate is over 98% for first-time applications.

Can you help if my visa application was previously rejected?

Yes, we specialize in handling previously refused cases. Our team will:

  1. Analyze the reasons for your previous rejection
  2. Address all issues in your new application
  3. Prepare a stronger case with additional supporting documents
  4. Provide guidance on any required appeals or reconsiderations

We have an 85% success rate with previously refused applications.

What makes your service different from applying directly?

Our service provides several key advantages:

  • Expert Guidance: Our consultants know the latest requirements and common pitfalls
  • Document Review: We check all documents for compliance before submission
  • Time Savings: We handle all paperwork and embassy communications
  • Higher Approval Rates: Properly prepared applications have much better success
  • Personalized Service: Dedicated consultant for your case
  • Updates & Tracking: Regular updates on your application status
