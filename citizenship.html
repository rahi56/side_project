Program Overview

The St. Lucia Citizenship by Investment Program was established in 2015 and offers investors and their families the opportunity to obtain St. Lucian citizenship through a government-approved investment. The program is known for its efficiency, transparency, and the high quality of its due diligence process.

St. Lucia is an English-speaking Caribbean nation with a stable democracy and strong economic growth. Citizenship provides visa-free access to key business and travel destinations including the UK, Schengen Zone, and Hong Kong.

Investment Options

  • National Economic Fund: $240,000 single applicant (minimum)
  • Real Estate: $300,000 minimum investment in approved projects
  • Government Bonds: $250,000 minimum investment (5-year holding)
  • Enterprise Project: $3.5 million minimum investment (joint ventures $6 million)

Requirements

  • Clean criminal record
  • Proof of legitimate source of funds
  • Good health
  • Minimum age of 18 years

Program Overview

The St. Kitts & Nevis Citizenship by Investment Program is the world's longest-running economic citizenship program, established in 1984. It offers investors and their families the opportunity to obtain citizenship through either a contribution to the Sustainable Island State Contribution (SISC) or through real estate investment.

This program is renowned for its prestige, strict due diligence, and excellent global mobility. St. Kitts & Nevis passport holders enjoy visa-free or visa-on-arrival access to the UK, EU Schengen zone, Russia, and many other important business and travel destinations.

Investment Options

  • Sustainable Island State Contribution: $250,000 single applicant
  • Real Estate:US $250,000 minimum investment in approved projects (must be held for 7 years)
  • Public Benefit Option: $250,000 contribution to approved public good projects

Requirements

  • Clean criminal record
  • Comprehensive due diligence check
  • Proof of legitimate source of funds
  • Medical certificate
  • Minimum age of 18 years

Key Benefits

  • Lifetime citizenship that can be passed to future generations
  • No residency or visit requirements
  • No worldwide income, wealth, or inheritance taxes
  • Ability to include spouse, children, parents and grandparents in application

Program Overview

The Grenada Citizenship by Investment Program was relaunched in 2013 with enhanced regulations and has become particularly attractive for investors seeking access to the United States through the E-2 investor visa treaty. Grenada is one of the few Caribbean countries with this US treaty relationship.

Grenada offers a stable political environment, beautiful natural scenery, and a growing economy focused on tourism and agriculture. The citizenship provides excellent visa-free travel options including China, Russia, and the EU Schengen zone.

Investment Options

  • National Transformation Fund: US $235,000 (family up to 4), plus per-dependant increments (e.g. $25 k/child, $50 k/adult), siblings $75 k
  • Real Estate: US $270,000 (shared) or $350,000 (sole), plus $50k government fee (for up to 4 people)

Requirements

  • Clean criminal record
  • Proof of legitimate source of funds
  • Medical certificate
  • Minimum age of 18 years

Key Benefits

  • Eligibility to apply for US E-2 investor visa
  • Visa-free access to China and Russia
  • No worldwide income tax for non-residents
  • Commonwealth citizenship with right to live in the UK for 6 months
