Frequently Asked Questions

What types of government processes can you assist with?

We assist with various official procedures including visa applications, residency permits, business registrations, document legalization, and other bureaucratic processes that don't require legal representation.

How is your service different from hiring a lawyer?

We provide procedural guidance and administrative support, while lawyers provide legal advice and representation. We help you navigate processes, while lawyers interpret laws and represent you in legal matters.

Can you guarantee approval of my applications?

No. While we ensure your applications are complete and properly prepared, final decisions always rest with the relevant government authorities.

Do you handle document translation services?

We coordinate with professional translation services and can arrange certified translations when needed, but we don't provide translation services directly.

What if I need actual legal advice?

For matters requiring legal advice, we maintain a network of qualified attorneys and can refer you to appropriate legal professionals based on your needs.
