Immigration FAQs

How long does the immigration process typically take?
Processing times vary significantly depending on the country, visa type, and individual circumstances. Simple tourist visas may take weeks, while residency applications can take several months to years. During our consultation, we provide realistic timelines for your specific case.
What documents will I need to provide?
Requirements vary by visa type and destination country but typically include passport, photographs, financial documents, health certificates, and sometimes police clearance. We provide a customized checklist after assessing your case.
Can you help if my visa application was previously refused?
Yes, we specialize in overcoming visa refusals. We analyze refusal reasons, address deficiencies, and strengthen your new application. Many clients come to us after unsuccessful attempts elsewhere.
Do you work with investment immigration programs?
Absolutely. We're experienced with Golden Visas, citizenship by investment, and entrepreneur immigration programs worldwide. We help select the right program for your budget and goals while ensuring full compliance.
How are your fees structured?
We offer transparent pricing with options for fixed-fee packages (for straightforward cases) or hourly rates (for complex matters). Government fees are always separate. After our consultation, we provide a detailed fee estimate with no obligations.
